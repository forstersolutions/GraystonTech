VerityForge

The command center above coding agents, proof, and release control.

VerityForge is a local-first autonomous development control plane. It turns plain-English owner intent into duration-bound missions, proof contracts, isolated worktrees, checks, questions, suggestions, evidence packages, and human-gated release decisions.

  • Agent router for Codex, Claude-style agents, custom local runners, IDE handoffs, and cloud-agent routes
  • Proof ledger for contracts, forge runs, candidates, gates, questions, suggestions, logs, and artifacts
  • Release-gate evaluations that join local checks, GitHub PR/CI state, review evidence, and policy blockers
  • OpenClaw power-lane support for supervised scheduling, sandbox execution, health checks, and dedicated worker phases when a mission needs tighter control
  • Commercial roadmap and value-driver reporting for software output, workflow integrations, trust, risk, and system-of-record pull
OpenClaw lives inside VerityForge.

It is the optional supervised runtime lane, not the headline product. VerityForge owns the mission, route, proof contract, evidence, risk ceiling, and human release gate.

MyPokerMaps / StackTrax / FeltOS

Live poker discovery connected to a full operator operating system.

MyPokerMaps is live at www.mypokermaps.com. The public product handles room discovery, club profiles, tournaments, live table availability, streaming discovery, social/player features, and verified operator onboarding.

  • Map-first room discovery with city/ZIP search and radius controls
  • Public navigation for clubs, tournaments, live streaming, and social hub flows
  • Club listing, claim, onboarding, and operator portal paths
  • FeltOS command layer for floor operations, tables, waitlists, tournaments, members, staff, cash cage, tablets, displays, wallet policy, and public-web controls
FeltOS is the operator layer inside the MyPokerMaps ecosystem.

Public discovery gets players to the room. FeltOS gives the operator a signed-in club portal for the room: floor state, table action, waitlist movement, tournament lifecycle, member identity, staff workflow, time collection, cash movement, displays, and public-facing policy switches.

FeltOS

The poker-room operating layer behind MyPokerMaps.

FeltOS turns MyPokerMaps from a discovery product into a signed-in club operations system. It is built for operators who need floor state, waitlists, member identity, cash workflows, tournament clocks, staff permissions, displays, and public listings to agree everywhere.

  • Real club-portal modules for dashboard, check-in, waitlist, floor, tables, tablets, tournaments, members, promotions, cash cage, player funds, accounting, analytics, compliance, employees, and settings
  • Canonical table configuration drives stakes, waitlist grouping, TV cards, dealer tablet behavior, and front-desk workflows
  • Membership-first controls connect dues, balances, receipts, wallet status, loyalty, history, check-in, holds, and player self-service
  • Cash-cage and time-collection workflows support buy-ins, cash-outs, drawer sessions, player vaults, marker controls, reports, and operational audit trails
  • Launch discipline for provisioning, module scoping, staff permissions, public profile readiness, display publishing, controlled rollout, and support fallback paths
